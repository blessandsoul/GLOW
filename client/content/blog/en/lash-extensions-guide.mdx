<h2 id="what-are-lash-extensions">What Are Lash Extensions?</h2>

<p>Lash extensions are a beauty procedure where synthetic fibers are attached individually or in fans to your natural lashes. The result — longer, fuller, and more expressive eyes without daily makeup.</p>

<h2 id="types">Types of Lash Extensions</h2>

<h3 id="classic">Classic Extensions</h3>

<p>With the classic method, one synthetic fiber is attached to each natural lash. This creates a natural, elegant look. Ideal for those who want to enhance their natural beauty.</p>

<h3 id="volume">Volume (2D-6D)</h3>

<p>Volume technique attaches 2 to 6 thin fibers to each natural lash. This creates a dramatic, voluminous effect. Perfect for special occasions or everyday expressive eyes.</p>

<h3 id="mega-volume">Mega Volume (10D+)</h3>

<p>The most dramatic option that creates maximum volume. Recommended only by experienced masters, as it requires high professionalism.</p>

<h2 id="aftercare">How to Care for Them?</h2>

<p>The longevity of lash extensions largely depends on proper aftercare. Here are the essential tips:</p>

<ul>
<li>Avoid water contact for the first 24 hours</li>
<li>Do not rub your eyes</li>
<li>Avoid oil-based products around the eye area</li>
<li>Brush daily with a special spoolie brush</li>
<li>Avoid sleeping face-down</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="how-long-do-they-last">How Long Do They Last?</h2>

<p>Well-done lash extensions last 3-4 weeks. After that, a fill appointment is recommended, which takes less time than a full set.</p>
