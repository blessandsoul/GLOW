# Glow.GE Terms of Service

Glow is a marketplace and the merchant collecting online card booking payments through Flitt. Independent Masters perform the booked services and remain responsible for quality, licensing and taxes. Manual-payment bookings are settled directly with the Master; a failed card checkout never silently changes to manual.

The service, price, time, payment channel and cancellation-fee snapshot shown at booking are binding. Online cancellations follow the published Refund Policy: Master cancellation and client cancellation at least 24 hours before the appointment receive a full refund; a later client cancellation or no-show retains only the snapshotted deposit.

Glow currently charges no booking commission and pays the Master 100% of net booking funds by a manually recorded bank transfer. Completed-booking funds become eligible after 24 hours; retained cancellation fees become eligible 24 hours after the scheduled appointment. A later refund or chargeback is offset against future payouts. Full bank-account details remain outside Glow.

Glow may reconcile payment-provider records, safely retry uncertain refunds, correct ledger errors, defend chargebacks and suspend abuse. Card numbers are handled by the payment provider and not stored by Glow. Georgian law applies. Contact: support@glow.ge.
