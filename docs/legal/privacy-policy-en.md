# Glow.GE Privacy Policy — booking and payments addendum

Glow processes account and verification data; booking parties, service, schedule, notes and cancellation records; payment/refund identifiers, amounts in minor units, currency and status; master-ledger and payout references; and security/audit logs. These records support contract performance, payment collection and refund, fraud prevention, reconciliation, accounting, chargeback defence and legal duties.

Necessary data may be shared with the booking parties, Flitt/banking partners, communications and infrastructure providers, advisers and authorities. Glow does not store full card numbers or full Master bank-account/IBAN details. Financial audit data is retained for applicable Georgian tax, accounting, limitation and anti-fraud periods; other data is deleted or anonymised when no longer needed. Secure management tokens are stored as irreversible hashes.

Users may exercise applicable access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection and portability rights via support@glow.ge. Mandatory retention may limit deletion.
