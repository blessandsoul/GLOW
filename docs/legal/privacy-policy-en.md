# Privacy Policy — Glow.GE

**Effective date:** March 5, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

1.1. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how the Glow.GE platform ("Platform", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects the personal data of its users ("you").

1.2. We take data protection seriously and process your data in accordance with the data protection laws of Georgia.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1. Data you provide:
- **Registration data:** phone number, first name, last name
- **Profile data:** profile photo, description, contact information
- **Uploaded content:** photos submitted for processing
- **Payment data:** information required for payment processing (handled by our payment provider; we do not store bank card details)
- **Support requests:** content of your messages to our team

### 2.2. Data collected automatically:
- **Technical information:** device type, operating system, browser version
- **Usage data:** dates and times of visits, pages viewed, features used
- **Cookies:** for session management and service improvement

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

3.1. We use the collected data for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Providing services (photo processing, account management) | Contract performance |
| Quality control and content moderation | Legitimate interest |
| Sending informational and promotional messages | Your consent |
| Improving and developing the Platform | Legitimate interest |
| Security and fraud prevention | Legitimate interest |
| Compliance with legal requirements | Legal obligation |

3.2. We do not use your photos for training artificial intelligence models, advertising, or any other purposes not specified in this Policy.

---

## 4. Moderation and Content Access

4.1. Photos uploaded to the Platform may be reviewed by our team to:
- verify processing quality;
- ensure compliance with Platform standards;
- identify prohibited content.

4.2. Access to images is strictly limited to authorized employees who have signed non-disclosure agreements (NDA).

4.3. Review is conducted only within the Platform's operational workflows. Staff members are not permitted to copy, download, or distribute your content.

---

## 5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

5.1. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes.

5.2. We may share data only with:
- **Payment providers** — for payment processing
- **Technical contractors** — for hosting and infrastructure maintenance (bound by confidentiality obligations)
- **Government authorities** — when required by law or court order

---

## 6. Data Retention

6.1. We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services or fulfill legal obligations:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account data | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Uploaded photos | Until order completion + 90 days |
| Processed photos | Until download + 30 days |
| Payment history | 3 years (legal requirement) |
| Usage logs | 12 months |

6.2. After account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days, except data required to be retained by law.

---

## 7. Data Security

7.1. We implement the following measures to protect your data:
- Data encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Least-privilege access controls
- Regular backups
- Suspicious activity monitoring

7.2. Despite all measures, no system can guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach, we will notify you as promptly as possible.

---

## 8. Your Rights

8.1. You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data (account deletion)
- **Restrict** data processing
- **Withdraw consent** for promotional communications at any time
- **Obtain a copy** of your data in a machine-readable format

8.2. To exercise your rights, contact us at support@glow.ge. We will respond within 15 business days.

---

## 9. Cookies

9.1. We use the following types of cookies:

| Type | Purpose | Required |
|------|---------|----------|
| Essential | Authentication, security, session management | Mandatory |
| Analytics | Service improvement, usage statistics | With your consent |

9.2. You can manage cookies in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect Platform functionality.

---

## 10. Communications and Marketing

10.1. By registering, you consent to receiving:
- **Service notifications** (order status, account changes) — always sent, necessary for service operation
- **Promotional messages** (offers, new features, deals) — can be disabled in settings

10.2. You can opt out of promotional messages:
- In your account settings
- Via the "Unsubscribe" link in any message
- By contacting us at support@glow.ge

---

## 11. Children

11.1. The Platform is not intended for persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn that we have received a child's data, we will delete it immediately.

---

## 12. Changes to This Policy

12.1. We may update this Policy. You will be notified of significant changes through the Platform or via your contact details.

12.2. We recommend periodically reviewing this Policy for updates.

---
