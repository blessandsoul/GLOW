# Refund Policy — Glow.GE

**Effective date:** March 5, 2026

---

## 1. General Provisions

1.1. This Refund Policy describes the conditions under which a user may receive a refund for photo editing services on the Glow.GE platform.

1.2. We value every client and strive to exceed your expectations. If something went wrong, we're here to make it right.

---

## 2. Full Refund

2.1. You are entitled to a full refund if:

- **Order not fulfilled** — we did not deliver the result within the specified timeframe
- **Technical error** — a payment glitch caused a duplicate charge
- **Service unavailable** — the platform was down and you were unable to receive your result

---

## 3. Partial Refund or Free Revision

3.1. If you are not satisfied with the result, we offer your choice of:

- **Free revision** — tell us what needs to be adjusted and we'll redo the work
- **Partial refund** — if a revision is not feasible or does not resolve the issue

3.2. To request a revision or partial refund:
- Contact us within **7 days** of receiving the result
- Describe what did not meet your expectations
- Attach the original and the result for comparison

---

## 4. Refunds Are Not Provided

4.1. Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

- **"Just didn't like it"** without specific feedback — after a free revision was offered and declined
- **More than 7 days** have passed since receiving the result
- **Photo already used** — the edited photo has been published or used commercially
- **Policy violation** — account was blocked for violating the Terms of Service
- **Poor source material** — the uploaded photo was too low quality to produce a proper result (we will notify you before starting work)

---

## 5. How to Request a Refund

5.1. Contact us through any convenient channel:
- In-app support chat
- Email: support@glow.ge
- Telegram: @glowge_support

5.2. Please include:
- Order number
- Reason for the request
- What specifically did not meet your expectations (screenshots are helpful)

5.3. We will review your request within **3 business days** and inform you of the decision.

---

## 6. Refund Method

6.1. Refunds are issued via the same payment method used for the original purchase.

6.2. Processing time depends on the payment provider and may take up to 10 business days.

---

## 7. Our Guarantee

7.1. We want you to be happy with the result. If the editing didn't meet expectations, we'll try to fix it first. A refund is a last resort when a fix truly isn't possible.

---

*For any questions, reach out to our support team — we're always here to help.*
