# Glow.GE Booking Refund Policy

This policy applies to online card bookings collected by Glow. Manual payments made directly to a Master are resolved with that Master.

- Master cancellation: refund 100% of the unreversed captured amount.
- Client cancellation at the exact 24-hour deadline or earlier: refund 100%.
- Client cancellation later than the deadline or no-show: refund the captured amount minus the deposit snapshotted when the booking was created.
- A refund is never negative and never exceeds the remaining captured amount.

The secure SMS link displays the exact quote before confirmation. Glow sends full or partial reversals to Flitt and the original card. Processing, failed and uncertain reversals stay in the admin reconciliation queue and can be retried idempotently. The issuing bank controls the final posting time. Support: support@glow.ge.
