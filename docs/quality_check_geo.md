### Tone Match

### Grammar Notes
- All spelled-out numbers in Russian ("три", "два", "пятьдесят") were converted to digits (3, 2, 50) per the Lang rule constraint "NUMBERS AS DIGITS".
- Strict substitution of synonyms to ensure no root word is used more than twice (except core terms like credit/platform).
- Short Russian segments were expanded slightly to avoid abrupt 2-word sentences in Georgian context.
