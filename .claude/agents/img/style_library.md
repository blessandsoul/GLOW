## 2. Lighting Types

### 2A. Natural & Ambient Light

| Lighting | Description | Best for |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Natural window light | Soft diffused light from a window, directional | Portraits, products, editorial |
| Golden hour | Warm low-angle sunlight, long shadows, amber tones | Outdoor portraits, landscapes, lifestyle |
| Blue hour | Cool pre-dawn or post-sunset ambient, soft blue tones | Moody exteriors, cityscapes |
| Overcast / cloudy | Even diffused skylight, no harsh shadows, cool neutral tone | Even portraits, product, documentary |
| Moonlight | Cool blue-silver ambient, low intensity | Night scenes, romance, quiet mood |
| Dappled light | Filtered through leaves/trees, spots of light and shadow | Outdoor portraits, nature, dreamy |
| Firelight / candlelight | Warm flickering amber, soft and directional | Intimacy, period pieces, warmth |
| Twilight | Dim ambient with color transition (warm horizon to cool sky) | Transitional mood, melancholy, time passage |

### 2B. Studio & Controlled Light

| Lighting | Description | Best for |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Studio three-point | Key + fill + back light setup | Commercial, product, controlled look |
| Rembrandt | Triangle of light on shadowed cheek, single source 45 degrees | Dramatic portraits, character studies |
| Split lighting | Light hits exactly half the face, hard line down center | High contrast portraits, mystery, duality |
| Butterfly / Paramount | Light directly above and in front, shadow under nose | Glamour, beauty, fashion editorial |
| Clamshell | Two soft sources above and below face, minimal shadow | Beauty, cosmetics, flattering skin |
| Beauty dish | Focused soft light with slight contrast edge, circular catchlight | Beauty, editorial portraits, skin texture |
| Ring light | Even frontal light from circular source, ring-shaped catchlight in eyes | Beauty, social media, even illumination |
| Strip light | Narrow rectangular source creating thin highlight bands | Product edges, rim accents, sleek surfaces |
| Soft light | Gradual shadow transitions, diffused source | Beauty, product, flattering portraits |
| Hard light | Sharp shadows, defined edges, direct source | Graphic compositions, noir, high contrast |

### 2C. Dramatic & Stylized Light

| Lighting | Description | Best for |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Rim / Edge lighting | Light from behind subject creating bright outline | Silhouettes, drama, subject separation |
| Top light | Light directly from above | Dramatic, theatrical, isolation |
| Under light | Light from below face | Horror, unease, supernatural |
| Chiaroscuro | Extreme contrast between light and dark, Renaissance style | Fine art, dramatic portraits, luxury |
| Silhouette | Subject completely backlit, no frontal light | Mystery, anonymity, graphic impact |
| Volumetric | Visible light rays through atmosphere (fog, dust, smoke) | Cinematic depth, mood, epic scenes |
| Neon / colored gel | Colored light sources, multiple hues | Cyberpunk, nightlife, music visuals |
| Cross light | Two opposing sources creating double shadows and edge highlights | Texture emphasis, drama, sculptural form |
| Gobo patterns | Light shaped by cutout (window blinds, branches, geometric) | Noir, atmosphere, patterned shadow play |
| Stage / concert lighting | Multiple colored spots, haze, dramatic beams, performance energy | Music visuals, live events, energy |
| Practical lighting | Light from visible sources in scene (lamps, screens, candles) | Realism, interior scenes, atmosphere |
| Fluorescent / clinical | Flat greenish-white overhead light | Unsettling realism, institutional, horror |
| Blacklight / UV | Ultraviolet glow, fluorescent colors pop, dark background | Party, surreal, neon accents, sci-fi |
| Spotlight pool | Single isolated beam in darkness, tight circle of light | Isolation, theater, interrogation, focus |
| Bounce light | Indirect reflected light from walls/surfaces, soft fill | Natural fill, realism, subtle dimension |

## 5. Quality & Technical Modifiers

### 5A. Realism & Resolution

| Modifier | What it tells the generator |
|----------|-----------------------------|
| photorealistic | Maximum realism, could be mistaken for a photograph |
| hyperrealistic | Beyond photo — visible pores, fabric threads, dust particles |
| 4K resolution | Ultra-sharp detail, standard high quality |
| 8K resolution | Maximum detail, large print quality, micro-textures visible |
| RAW photo | Unprocessed look, flat color profile, maximum detail retention |
| DSLR quality | Professional camera look, natural optics, realistic lens behavior |
| medium format | Larger sensor look, extreme shallow DOF falloff, tonal richness |
| micro-detail | Every tiny texture visible — pores, fibers, dust, scratches |

### 5B. Lens & Optics Effects

| Modifier | What it tells the generator |
|----------|-----------------------------|
| shallow depth of field | Sharp subject, blurred background (low f-stop feel) |
| deep depth of field | Everything sharp from foreground to background |
| bokeh | Soft circular highlights in out-of-focus areas |
| lens flare | Light artifacts from bright source hitting lens |
| chromatic aberration | Color fringing at edges, adds analog character |
| anamorphic | Horizontal lens flares, oval bokeh, widescreen distortion |
| tilt-shift | Selective focus band makes real scenes look miniature |
| barrel distortion | Slight outward curve at frame edges, wide-angle lens feel |
| fisheye | Extreme wide-angle spherical distortion, 180-degree view |
| vignette | Darkened corners, draws eye to center, vintage or cinematic feel |
| lens distortion | Subtle optical warping, adds realism and character |

### 5C. Film & Texture Effects

| Modifier | What it tells the generator |
|----------|-----------------------------|
| film grain | Organic analog texture, adds warmth and imperfection |
| motion blur | Streak from movement, conveys speed or time |
| long exposure | Light trails, smooth water, star trails, time compression |
| double exposure | Two images layered into one frame, ghostly overlap |
| cross-processed | Shifted color channels, unusual color cast, experimental |
| matte finish | Non-glossy surface quality, soft diffused look |
| glossy finish | High-shine reflective surface quality, saturated and vivid |
| wet surface | Reflective sheen on surfaces, rain-soaked, intensified colors and reflections |
| bloom / glow | Soft light bleed around bright areas, dreamy halo effect |
| halation | Red-orange glow around bright highlights, vintage film characteristic |

### 5D. Color & Tone Control

| Modifier | What it tells the generator |
|----------|-----------------------------|
| high contrast | Deep blacks, bright whites, minimal midtones |
| low contrast | Flat tones, muted range, soft atmospheric feel |
| desaturated | Reduced color intensity, muted, bleached feel |
| oversaturated | Pushed color intensity beyond natural, vivid and punchy |
| warm color grading | Shifted toward amber/orange tones, cozy inviting feel |
| cool color grading | Shifted toward blue/teal tones, clinical or melancholy |
| split toning | Different color cast in shadows vs highlights (e.g., blue shadows, warm highlights) |
| ACES film tone mapping | Cinema-standard color science, natural rolloff in highlights, rich midtones |
| color negative | Inverted colors with orange mask, surreal and experimental |

### 5E. 3D & Render Quality

| Modifier | What it tells the generator |
|----------|-----------------------------|
| ray tracing | Physically accurate light bounces, reflections, and refractions |
| subsurface scattering | Light penetrates translucent surfaces (skin, wax, leaves, marble) |
| ambient occlusion | Soft shadow accumulation in crevices and contact points, adds depth |
| global illumination | Realistic indirect light bouncing between surfaces, natural fill |
| caustics | Focused light patterns from refraction through glass or water |
| volumetric rendering | 3D light rays visible in atmospheric medium (fog, dust, smoke) |
| PBR materials | Physically-based rendering — realistic surface response to light |
| displacement mapping | Surface geometry deformed by texture, real depth not just visual trick |
